Sensitive Information
Dangers
Consequences of disclosing sensitive personal information:
Identity Theft
When someone steals your identity, they utilise your personal information to pose as you in public places, such as online forums or social media. They can perpetrate many sorts of mischief, including financial fraud, by adopting your online identity, and they can even publish stuff on your social media accounts that might ruin your reputation.
Financial theft
If you give out information such as your or your parents' credit card number, there is a risk of unauthorised withdrawals, purchases and transfers, i.e. theft of money
